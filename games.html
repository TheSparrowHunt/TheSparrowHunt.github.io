Games

Gravity Frames

Gravity Frames is a single screen platformer made for GBJAM5 over a period of 10 days in October 2016. This game was made with GameMaker: Studio. Versions are available for Windows, macOS and Linux. There is also a download available for the music in the game in both MP3 and WAV format.


Available on: itch.io

Crowd Pleaser

Crowd Pleaser is a game about waving to a crowd, made over the period of two days with Alice Casey for Global Game Jam 2017. This game was made with GameMaker: Studio and is playable in browser (on machines with keyboard inputs).


Available on: itch.io
